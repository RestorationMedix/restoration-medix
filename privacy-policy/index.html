Privacy Policy

How Restoration Medix LLC collects, uses, and protects your information when you visit our website or request our services.

Last updated: June 10, 2026

1. Who We Are

Restoration Medix LLC ("Restoration Medix," "we," "us," or "our") is a licensed general contractor based in Auburn, Washington, providing water damage restoration, mold remediation, fire damage restoration, reconstruction, remodeling, and roofing services across the greater Seattle area, South King County, and Pierce County.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you visit restorationmedix.com (the "Site") or contact us for services. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you submit our estimate request form or contact us, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Property ZIP code
  • Service type requested (e.g., water damage, mold remediation, roofing)
  • Any additional details you choose to share about your property or project

Information collected automatically

When you browse the Site, our analytics and advertising tools automatically collect:

  • Pages visited, time on page, and how you arrived at the Site (e.g., search, ad click)
  • Approximate location (city/region level), derived from your IP address
  • Device and browser type, screen size, and operating system
  • Cookie identifiers and advertising identifiers (see Sections 4 and 5)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your request — when you submit the estimate form, we use your name, phone number, and email to call or text you back about your project, usually within minutes.
  • To dispatch and perform services — your ZIP code and service type help us route the right crew to your property.
  • To support insurance claims — if you engage us for covered work, we may use your project information to prepare documentation for your insurance carrier, with your knowledge.
  • To measure and improve marketing — we use analytics and conversion data to understand which pages and ads bring customers to us.
  • To operate and secure the Site — including spam prevention and troubleshooting.

By submitting the estimate request form, you consent to Restoration Medix contacting you by phone, text message, and email regarding your request. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out of text messages at any time by replying STOP.

4. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. The Site uses cookies and similar technologies set by Google for two purposes:

  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics 4) — to understand how visitors use the Site in aggregate.
  • Advertising cookies (Google Ads) — to measure conversions from our ads and, where applicable, support remarketing.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not prevent you from using the Site or requesting an estimate.

5. Analytics & Advertising

Google Analytics 4 (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect aggregated usage statistics about the Site. GA4 may collect your IP address, device information, and browsing behavior on the Site. Google processes this data on our behalf under its own privacy terms. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google Ads conversion tracking

We use Google Ads conversion tracking to measure when a visitor who clicked one of our ads submits an estimate request. This involves cookies and identifiers set by Google. You can manage ad personalization at Google Ads Settings or learn more at How Google uses information from sites that use its services.

6. Third-Party Service Providers

We share information only with service providers that help us run our business:

  • GoHighLevel (HighLevel Inc.) — our customer relationship management (CRM) platform. Estimate form submissions are transmitted securely through our website server to GoHighLevel so our team can call you back and manage your project.
  • Google (Alphabet Inc.) — analytics and advertising measurement, as described in Section 5.
  • Netlify, Inc. — our website hosting provider, which processes form submissions and standard server logs.
  • Insurance carriers — if you hire us for insurance-covered work, we share project documentation (photos, moisture readings, scope of work) with your carrier as part of the claim process.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

We may also disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our legal rights.

7. Washington State Privacy Rights

Restoration Medix is a Washington business serving Washington residents, and we comply with applicable Washington State law, including the Washington Consumer Protection Act.

We do not collect "consumer health data" as defined by the Washington My Health My Data Act (RCW 19.373). Our services concern property restoration, and our lead form does not request health information. If you voluntarily share health-related details with us (for example, mold sensitivity concerns), we use them only to plan and perform your restoration work and do not sell or share them.

Washington residents may contact us at any time to:

  • Ask what personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or delete your personal information
  • Withdraw consent to marketing communications

To exercise these rights, call (206) 557-6444. We will respond to verified requests within a reasonable time and will not discriminate against you for exercising them.

8. Data Retention & Security

We retain lead and customer information for as long as needed to serve you, comply with legal and insurance documentation requirements, and maintain business records. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards — including encrypted (HTTPS) transmission of form submissions — to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children's Privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Material changes will be posted on this page.
