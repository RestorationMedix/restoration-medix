Tacoma FAQ

Water damage questions,

Do you serve all Tacoma neighborhoods, including the North End and Stadium District?
Yes — from the North End, Stadium District, and Proctor to Hilltop, South Tacoma, and the East Side. We respond across the entire city, 24/7.
Tacoma has a lot of older homes — is aging plumbing a common cause of water damage?
Very. Much of Tacoma's housing predates 1950, and original galvanized plumbing, supply lines, and older roofs are frequent sources of leaks and sudden bursts. In an older home, a small leak can become a big loss quickly.
Can you work directly with my Tacoma insurance adjuster?
Yes. We document the loss with photos, moisture readings, and drying logs to carrier standards and bill your insurer directly, so most Tacoma homeowners only pay their deductible.
